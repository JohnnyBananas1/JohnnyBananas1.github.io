Lilith's Game

Co-Producer

"Lilith's Game" is the story of couple Hunter and Lily as they discover the secrets of a mysterious video-game that appears on their doorstep. As Hunter becomes enthralled with the game, his line between reality and the video-game begins to blur.
This short film was created for a film production course at Notre Dame with the brutal requirements of telling an entire story without dialogue and without recording sound on set.

Out of the Ashes

Co-Producer

The residents of Mannford, Oklahoma try to cope with life, four months after a wildfire destroys their town. The devastated town is struggling to provide necessities for its troubled residents. Families live in homemade storage sheds and remember the tangible things they once cherished.

Saints and Swimmers

Producer

Saints and Swimmers is a charitable program that teaches Hispanic children to swim. I have filmed, edited, and produced promotional videos to help recruit highschool students to teach children water-safety.

n00b

Co-Producer

"n00b" is the story of Lego enthusiast Ben being dragged along for a suspicious set of errands with his old delinquent childhood friend Travis. Some milestones of their journey include a massive tray of hash-brownies, a street shooting and envelopes full of cash.
